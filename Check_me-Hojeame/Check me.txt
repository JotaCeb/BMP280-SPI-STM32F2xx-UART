The software is programed in 4 wire configuration.
		SPI1 GPIO Configuration    
		PB3     ------> SPI1_SCK
    		PB4     ------> SPI1_MISO
    		PB5     ------> SPI1_MOSI 
    		PA4     ------> SPI1_NSS
The measurements are caught with normal mode, you will not need write "11" in mode[1:0] before acquisition.

Steps for perform a quickly test
1. Select oversampling temperature
2. Select oversampling pressure
3. Enter (I)

The result will be the lectures of the values inside the 6 registers OxF7, OxF8, OxF9, OxFA, OxFB, OxFC:
(P: 24 bits T: 24 bits)
P: 0xF7[7:0]F8[7:0]F9[7:0]                 T: FA[7:0]FB[7:0]FC[7:0]
